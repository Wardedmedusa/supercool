Stress-induced mass hysteria

This could have been an example of psychogenic movement disorder happening in mass hysteria or mass psychogenic illness. This involves many individuals suddenly exhibiting the same bizarre behaviour. This can be caused by high levels of psychological stress. This high level of stress could have been caused by the starvation and disease that was present in the region at the time.
Basically people got so stressed that their body decided to start uncontrollably dancing till they died. I don't know what kind of twisted fight or flight response that is but I hope it never happens to me.
